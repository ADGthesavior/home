About Me

Hi, I am Dr. Anirban Dasgupta, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering (EEE) at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, Assam, India. I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur.

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, signal processing, and embedded systems. I am actively working on applications in autonomous systems, driver monitoring, and AI-based vision systems.
